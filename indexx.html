Unit Conversions
Temperature Weight Distance
Temperature



To convert celsius to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the `C` with your temperature in celsius: (C × 9/5) + 32
Weight



To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the `kg` with your weight in kilograms: kg × 2.2
Distance



To convert kilometers to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the `km` with your distance in kilometers: km × 0.62137
